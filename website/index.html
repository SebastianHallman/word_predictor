Type faster with word predictions

Speedy Writer is a lightweight desktop overlay that suggests completions as you type, supports F1-F10 quick selection, and learns from your usage over time.

Free, open source and local, as accessibility software should be.

Download View Source

Features

What you get

  • Shows up to 10 predictions for your current word prefix.
  • Accept suggestions instantly with F1-F10 shortcuts.
  • Learns from your typing locally with no cloud required.
  • Customize colors, font, and background transparency.
  • Comes with wordlists for English and Swedish. Add your own wordlists with txt files.
